Mental Health Awareness in Workplaces

In this rapid technological advancement, individuals are venturing into challenging domains in various innovative industries .The progress made in various fields But in pursuit of achieving professional goals ,there is gradual decline in well-being, posing as a serious threat to their mental health. Thus there arises need for ways that can aid in early detection of mental health disorders developing in individuals and help in improving the quality of life.

About the Dataset

OSMI (Open Source Mental Illness) which is a non-profit organization whose motive is to raise awareness about mental health disorders.They teach the economic community how mental disorders influence the productivity of workers and contribute to making the workplace a safe and better place for employees.

The survey is designed gather data on the prevalence of mental health issues, how these issues are perceived, and the impact they have on employees and companies.
